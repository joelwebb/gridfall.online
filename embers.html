Embers

Discover rare, collectible embers that transform your gameplay. Slot them into abilities or equipment to unlock unique effects and create powerful combinations.

The Ember System

Embers are rare, collectible items that can be slotted into abilities or equipment to provide unique effects based on their elemental properties.

Ability Enhancement

Slot embers into your abilities to add elemental damage, special effects, or modify their behavior entirely.

Example: A Poison Ember slotted into a basic attack ability will imbue poison damage, causing enemies to take damage over time.

Equipment Protection

Insert embers into equipment slots to gain resistances and protective effects against various status conditions.

Example: The same Poison Ember inserted into armor equipment prevents the wearer from becoming poisoned by enemy attacks.

Discover Rare Embers

Fire Ember

A blazing ember radiating intense heat and flames.

In Abilities: Adds burning damage and area effects

In Equipment: Decreases fire-based damage

Water Ember

A flowing blue ember with the power of tides.

In Abilities: Adds healing effects and water damage

In Equipment: Decreases water-based damage

Lightning Ember

A crackling ember charged with electrical energy.

In Abilities: Chain lightning effects between enemies

In Equipment: Immunity to paralysis and stun

Poison Ember

A venomous green ember pulsing with toxic energy.

In Abilities: Adds poison damage over time

In Equipment: Prevents poison status effects

Shadow Ember

A dark ember that bends light and conceals presence.

In Abilities: Adds stealth and shadow damage

In Equipment: Decreases shadow-based damage

Void Ember

A mysterious ember that manipulates space itself.

In Abilities: Causes void-based damage

In Equipment: Nullifies magical effects

Time Ember

A rare ember that controls the flow of time.

In Abilities: Adds time manipulation effects

In Equipment: Immunity to temporal effects

Blood Ember

A crimson ember that pulses with vital energy.

In Abilities: Adds life stealing effects to attacks

In Equipment: Increases maximum HP and vitality

Create Unique Combinations

Find all the rare embers to unleash unique combos and create your own custom abilities!

🔥 + ⚡ = Plasma Burst

Combine Fire and Lightning embers to create devastating plasma attacks that deal massive damage and spread to nearby enemies.

💧 + ⚡ = Chain Frost

Mix Water and Lightning embers to chain frost damage to enemies, slowing them and leaping from any enemies within one adjacent space.
