Characters

Meet the legendary heroes fighting against the Lucient Choir. Each class brings unique abilities that can inflict severe damage, shield allies, or restore your team's strength.

Damage Dealer

Kestra the Emberweaver

A master of destructive ember magic, Kestra specializes in devastating area-of-effect abilities that can turn the tide of battle. Her flame-infused spells inflict severe damage to multiple enemies at once.

Inferno Strike

Unleashes a devastating fire wave that damages all enemies in a line

Ember Burst

Creates explosive ember mines that detonate when enemies approach

Phoenix Form

Transforms into a phoenix, gaining flight and enhanced fire abilities

Shield Bearer

Theron the Aegis Guard

The stalwart defender of the rebellion, Theron excels at protecting allies from harm. His ember-enhanced shields can absorb devastating attacks while providing protective barriers for the entire team.

Aegis Barrier

Creates an impenetrable shield that protects all nearby allies

```

Taunt

Forces enemies to attack him while reducing incoming damage

Guardian's Resolve

Becomes immovable and reflects damage back to attackers

Healer

Lyara the Lifebinder

The heart of any team, Lyara wields life-giving ember magic to keep allies fighting. Her powerful healing spells and regenerative abilities ensure the team can endure even the most challenging battles.

Mass Healing

Restores health to all allies and removes harmful status effects

Regeneration Aura

Creates a healing field that continuously restores ally health

Resurrection

Brings fallen allies back to life with partial health and ember

Legendary Character Abilities

Phoenix Incarnation

Hero: Kestra the Emberweaver

Type: Ultimate

Trigger: 25%

Effect Description

Kestra transforms into a blazing phoenix, gaining fire absorption, immunity to damage for 3 turns, and causing all her abilities to inflict massive fire damage in large areas. Upon transformation end, creates a devastating explosion.

Character Enhancement

With Phoenix Ember equipped: Transformation resurrects fallen allies.

Eternal Aegis

Hero: Theron the Aegis Guard

Type: Ultimate

Trigger: 25%

Effect Description

Creates an unbreakable shield dome that protects the entire party for 2 turns. While active, all damage is redirected to Theron, who becomes invulnerable and reflects 200% damage back to attackers.

Character Enhancement

With Guardian Ember equipped: Dome also heals allies and grants damage immunity after effect ends.

Genesis Bloom

Hero: Lyara the Lifebinder

Type: Ultimate

Trigger: 25%

Effect Description

Causes magical flowers to bloom across the battlefield, fully healing all allies and removing all debuffs.

Character Enhancement

With Life Ember equipped: Grants poison resistance and provides health regeneration of 10% for three turns to all players.
