Grid-Based Combat System

Experience tactical combat like never before with our innovative grid-based battle system inspired by legendary strategy games.

The Art of Pincer Attacks

Position your customizable party strategically on the battlefield grid. Victory comes to those who master the ancient technique of the pincer attack - surrounding enemies between two or more allies to unleash devastating combination strikes.

Each successful pincer triggers powerful ember-based abilities, turning the tide of battle with spectacular magical devastation.

Legendary Abilities

Discover some of the most powerful abilities available to heroes in their fight against the Lucient Choir.

Crimson Chains

Type: Offensive

Range: 3 tiles

Cost: 4 Ember

Cooldown: 3 turns

Effect Description

Conjures ethereal crimson chains that bind and damage enemies in a cross pattern. Chained enemies cannot move and take fire damage each turn. When used in a pincer attack, chains spread to additional enemies.

Ember Enhancement

With a Fire Ember slotted: Chains explode when they expire, dealing area damage.

Temporal Sanctuary

Type: Defensive

Range: 2 tile radius

Cost: 6 Ember

Cooldown: 5 turns

Effect Description

Creates a shimmering dome that slows time within its radius. Allies inside gain increased movement and can act twice per turn, while enemies are frozen in temporal stasis for 2 turns.

Ember Enhancement

With a Time Ember slotted: Sanctuary heals allies and cleanses all debuffs each turn.

Void Collapse

Type: Ultimate

Range: Entire battlefield

Cost: 10 Ember

Cooldown: Once per battle

Effect Description

Tears open a dimensional rift that pulls all enemies toward the center of the battlefield. Enemies take massive void damage based on how far they're pulled, and the rift remains for 3 turns, continuing to pull and damage.

Ember Enhancement

With a Void Ember slotted: Rift also banishes the strongest enemy for 2 turns, removing them from battle.
